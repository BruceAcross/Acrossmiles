About Across Miles

We started with a simple belief — that distance should never come in the way of showing love.

Why We Started

Built for Families That Are Miles Apart

Every day, people look for meaningful ways to show they care — whether it's celebrating a birthday, marking a special occasion, or simply brightening someone's day.

Sometimes you're just a few streets away. Other times, you're miles apart. But the intention remains the same.

That's where we come in. Across Miles is a hyperlocal gifting and care service that helps you send thoughtful surprises — from beautiful orchid bouquets to curated fruit baskets — directly to your loved ones' doorstep in Vasai West.

We’re not just about delivering gifts — we’re your extended hand, your personal touch, your way of showing you care, whether you're nearby or miles away.

Whether you're planning a surprise from across the world or picking out something special locally, we’re here to make it effortless and meaningful.

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about sending love through Across Miles.

How do I place an order?

+

You can place an order by messaging us on WhatsApp or through our contact form. Simply share your requirements, preferred date, and delivery details. We’ll confirm availability and guide you through the process.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept UPI and bank transfers. For customers abroad, international payment methods including PayPal and Wise can be arranged on request.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

+

Same-day delivery may be available depending on the product and timing of your request. We recommend placing your order at least 24 hours in advance for guaranteed availability.

Can I customize my order?

+

Yes, absolutely. Whether it's a specific bouquet style, a custom gift combination, or a special request, we’ll do our best to bring your idea to life.

What areas do you currently serve?

+

We currently serve Vasai West and nearby areas. If you're unsure whether we can deliver to a specific location, feel free to reach out.

How will I know the delivery was completed?

+

We confirm once your order has been successfully delivered, so you can be assured it has reached your loved ones.

Can you handle anniversaries and festivals?

+

Yes, we specialize in birthdays, anniversaries, festive gifting, and meaningful surprises. We can help you plan and deliver something truly special.

What if I need to change or cancel my order?

+

You can contact us as soon as possible to make changes or request a cancellation. Cancellations are free before order processing begins. Once items have been purchased or preparation has started, a cancellation fee may apply depending on the stage of the order.
